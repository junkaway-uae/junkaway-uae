Premium Furniture Disposal

Our team handles the heavy lifting for all major household furniture items. We specialize in the disassembly of large sectional sofas, king-size bedroom sets, and custom-built wardrobes. In Dubai, many apartment buildings have strict service elevator bookings. JunkAway Dubai coordinates with your building management to ensure we operate within the designated time slots, avoiding any fines or property damage. We use floor protection sheets for marble and wood flooring to ensure your property stays in pristine condition during the removal process.

Electronic & Appliance Recycling

Dubai produces a significant amount of E-waste annually. JunkAway is committed to the green initiative by ensuring all old refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers, and televisions are sent to certified recycling facilities in Al Quoz and Jebel Ali. We do not dump electronics in standard landfills. Each item is stripped for its core components (metals, plastics, circuit boards) to ensure 100% legal and environmental compliance with Dubai Municipality standards.

Commercial & Office Strip-Outs

For businesses in Business Bay and DIFC, moving offices can be a logistical nightmare. We provide overnight junk removal services to clear out old workstations, ergonomic chairs, server racks, and bulk filing cabinets. Our teams are trained in rapid-strip techniques, allowing us to clear a 5,000 sq ft office space in a single shift. We provide a full certificate of disposal for your corporate records, ensuring you meet your company's sustainability and waste management targets.
