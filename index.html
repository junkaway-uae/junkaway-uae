About JunkAway UAE

JunkAway UAE is one of the most established and trusted junk removal companies operating across Dubai and the entire UAE. Since our founding in 2008, we have built a strong reputation as a reliable, professional and eco-conscious waste management service provider. Dubai is one of the fastest growing cities in the world, with continuous residential upgrades, commercial expansion, luxury villa renovations, office relocations, and construction projects. All of these activities generate large volumes of unwanted junk, furniture waste, scrap materials and electronic waste. Our company was built specifically to solve this challenge with a structured logistics system that ensures fast, safe and environmentally responsible removal of all types of junk.
We operate a full fleet of specialized junk removal trucks, trained manpower teams, and advanced routing systems that allow us to provide same-day or next-day service across Dubai. We follow strict UAE Municipality waste management guidelines and ensure that every item collected is sorted for recycling, reuse or safe disposal. Our mission is not only to remove junk, but to reduce landfill waste and contribute to a cleaner and more sustainable Dubai.
We handle: Residential junk removal, commercial clearance, industrial waste, construction debris, office strip-outs, villa cleanouts, appliance disposal and eco recycling. Every project is handled with professionalism, safety standards and proper equipment to ensure no damage to property or surrounding areas.
