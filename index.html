About JunkAway UAE

Founded in 2008, JunkAway UAE was created to solve one of the fastest-growing problems in the region — unmanaged junk accumulation in residential and commercial properties. Dubai’s fast-paced development, real estate turnover, and renovation cycles create continuous waste challenges. Our company was designed to handle this with a structured, professional, and scalable junk removal system. We operate a fleet of specialized junk removal trucks, trained manpower teams, and eco-processing workflows that ensure every item collected is handled responsibly.
Our teams are trained to work inside high-rise buildings, gated communities, villas, warehouses, and commercial towers. We coordinate with building management, security, and municipality rules to ensure smooth execution.
